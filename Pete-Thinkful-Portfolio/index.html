Pete's Background

After graduating college, I became an art teacher and worked with beginners and professionals. I love art and my works have been featured in major galleries across the globe.

If you're looking to hire an artisit or you're an artist looking for a collaborator for your next project, please reach out! I'm so excited to work with other artists to create new art.
